Tartan to Tartan

A community that connects Tartans, built by Tartans.

Meet the Tartans that make CMU what it is.


Join us in welcoming our newest class, the Class of 2019+! Every week, Tartan to Tartan's Mentorship Program matches current and incoming Students based on their activities and interests. Using these methods, we can learn from each other and join in on meeting and welcoming the new freshmen first-hand.
